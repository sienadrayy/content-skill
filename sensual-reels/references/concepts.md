# Concept Library

Fresh concept ideas for daily script generation. Mix and match these elements to create infinite variations.

## Bedroom Concepts

### Luxury Bedroom Mood
- **Styling:** Silk robe, minimal underneath, jewelry
- **Angles:** Side profile in soft light, then turn to camera
- **Lighting:** Golden lamp, rim lighting on back
- **Poses:** Lying back, slow sit up, confident gaze
- **Vibe:** Luxe, intimate, confident

### Confidence in Minimal
- **Styling:** Sports bra or crop top, high-waisted bottoms
- **Angles:** Direct to camera, 45° side angle
- **Lighting:** Window light, soft and natural
- **Poses:** Stretch, shoulder back pose, direct eye contact
- **Vibe:** Empowered, sexy, assured

### Morning Glance
- **Styling:** Oversized shirt (minimal underneath visible), messy hair
- **Angles:** Overhead or at eye level, looking up/down
- **Lighting:** Natural window light, bedroom casual
- **Poses:** Shoulder stretch, hair flip, knowing smile
- **Vibe:** Effortlessly sexy, relatable, hot

## Bathroom/Vanity Concepts

### Pre-Shower Confidence
- **Styling:** Towel, minimal coverage
- **Angles:** Mirror selfie style, side profile in doorway
- **Lighting:** Bathroom lighting, bright but flattering
- **Poses:** Shoulder movement, towel adjustment, mirror gaze
- **Vibe:** Playful, sensual, authentic

### Mirror Moment
- **Styling:** Sports bra/bikini top, high-waisted anything
- **Angles:** Camera faces mirror reflection, then turn to face camera
- **Lighting:** Vanity lights if available, otherwise bathroom light
- **Poses:** Pose in mirror, turn around to face camera directly
- **Vibe:** Self-assured, proud, confident

## Outdoor/Golden Hour Concepts

### Golden Hour Strut
- **Styling:** Minimal crop top, high-waisted bottoms, sunglasses optional
- **Angles:** Tracking shot following slow walk, then still shot
- **Lighting:** Natural golden hour (sunset/sunrise)
- **Poses:** Confident walk, pause to look back, shoulder gaze
- **Vibe:** Carefree, confident, beautiful

### Sunset Body
- **Styling:** Bikini top, minimal shorts, no shoes
- **Angles:** Backlit silhouette, then front-facing in golden light
- **Lighting:** Golden hour sun + natural backlighting
- **Poses:** Stretch arms, slow spin, confident standing pose
- **Vibe:** Free, sexy, vacation energy

### Terrace/Balcony Moment
- **Styling:** Lingerie, robe, or minimal outfit
- **Angles:** Wide shot showing location, then close-up on body/face
- **Lighting:** Golden hour side lighting, cityscape/nature behind
- **Poses:** Lean on railing, look out then back to camera, confident stance
- **Vibe:** Luxe, wanderlust, sensual

## Fashion/Styling Concepts

### Texture Play
- **Styling:** Silk, satin, mesh, or lace top - mix textures
- **Angles:** Close-up of texture, then wider to show fit
- **Lighting:** Light that catches texture and sheen
- **Poses:** Movement to show how fabric flows, drapes on body
- **Vibe:** Luxury, tactile, sophisticated

### Minimalist Power
- **Styling:** Single color, minimal jewelry, understated luxury
- **Angles:** Full body, then 45° side profile
- **Lighting:** Even, flattering light to highlight silhouette
- **Poses:** Strong stance, confident posture, direct gaze
- **Vibe:** Elegant, powerful, hot

### Jewelry Focus
- **Styling:** Minimal clothing, statement jewelry (necklace, body chains, anklets)
- **Angles:** Close-up of jewelry placement, then wider
- **Lighting:** Light that catches jewelry, creates sparkle
- **Poses:** Movements that show jewelry in motion, hand touches
- **Vibe:** Glam, luxe, eye-catching

## Movement Concepts

### Slow Body Roll
- **Hook:** Direct eye contact, confident smile
- **Build:** Slow roll from one side to front
- **Main:** Full body roll, showing curves in motion
- **Engagement:** End facing camera, hold gaze
- **Vibe:** Sensual, controlled, hot

### Hair Movement
- **Hook:** Hair up or to the side
- **Build:** Start to move hair slowly
- **Main:** Full hair flip or hair toss, dramatic moment
- **Engagement:** Hair lands, look at camera
- **Vibe:** Playful, sexy, energetic

### Clothing Adjustment
- **Hook:** Static confident pose
- **Build:** Reach toward clothing deliberately
- **Main:** Slow adjust of top/hem/strap, reveal moment
- **Engagement:** Pull away, confident look
- **Vibe:** Sensual, teasing, confident

### Rotation/Spin
- **Hook:** Face the camera directly
- **Build:** Start slow rotation away
- **Main:** Full rotation showing back, sides, all angles
- **Engagement:** Complete rotation back to face camera
- **Vibe:** Complete package, confident, versatile

## Usage Instructions

When generating scripts, randomly choose:
1. A location/concept type
2. A styling pattern
3. A lighting mood
4. A primary pose/movement
5. A vibe

Combine them into a unique script that feels fresh every day.

**Don't repeat the same exact concept twice in a week.** Rotate through different location types, poses, and moods to keep content fresh while maintaining the hot, sensual aesthetic.
