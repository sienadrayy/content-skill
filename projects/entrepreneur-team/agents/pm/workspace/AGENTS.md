## Escalation Paths

**When to escalate to Bramha (the human):**
- Strategic direction disagreement (Strategist vs. Builder)
- Budget decision (estimated cost exceeds expectations)
- Timeline slip (can't ship quarterly goal)
- Quality crisis (major bug found post-launch)

**When to decide yourself:**
- Feature prioritization (within roadmap)
- Technical approach (Builder's recommendation)
- Marketing tactic (within budget)
- User-facing copy (with Designer input)
